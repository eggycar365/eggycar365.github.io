About Moto X3M

Moto X3M is an electrifying dirt bike racing game that delivers heart-pounding action and tests your reflexes across 22 thrilling levels. Developed by MadPuffers, this fast-paced game, first released in 2016, challenges players to race against the clock on treacherous tracks filled with loops, ramps, spikes, and explosive traps.

Each level in Moto X3M presents unique obstacles, from gravity-defying jumps to deadly hazards, requiring a perfect blend of speed, precision, and timing. Your goal is to reach the finish line as quickly as possible to earn up to three stars per level, with faster times yielding higher ratings. Performing mid-air stunts like front and back flips shaves 0.5 seconds off your time, boosting your score but demanding careful landings to avoid crashes. Stars earned can be used to unlock new bikes, adding variety and motivation to replay levels for better times.

How to play Moto X3M

Game Controls

  • Up Arrow/W: Accelerate.
  • Down Arrow/S: Brake.
  • Left Arrow/A, Right Arrow/D: Balance the bike for flips or stability.

Perform Stunts: Execute front or back flips mid-air to reduce your time by 0.5 seconds per flip.

Avoid Obstacles: Navigate ramps, loops, spikes, and traps without crashing.

Earn Stars: Complete levels quickly to earn up to three stars and unlock new bikes.

Replay for Mastery: Memorize tracks and practice to improve your times and scores.

Tips for Success

  • Balance speed and caution to avoid crashes that add seconds to your time.
  • Master flips on jumps to cut time, but ensure safe landings.
  • Learn each track’s layout to anticipate obstacles and optimize your route.
  • Use brakes strategically on steep slopes or before tricky traps.

What is Moto X3M?

A high-speed dirt bike racing game with 22 levels of challenging tracks, where you perform stunts and race against the clock to earn stars.

How do I get three stars?

Finish levels quickly, perform flips to reduce time, and avoid crashes to meet the level’s target time.

With 22 levels of increasing difficulty, unlockable bikes, and endless replay value, Moto X3M offers a thrilling ride for anyone ready to tackle its wild tracks and chase those coveted three-star ratings.
